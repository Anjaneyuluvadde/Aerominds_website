Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the AeroDroneMitra ecosystem.

Farmers and Vendors can visit our official website at aerodronemitra.com to download the official Android application package (.APK) or access links to the Google Play Store. Registration requires a verified mobile number via OTP verification.

To register as a service vendor, you must upload a valid DGCA Remote Pilot Certificate (RPC), proof of drone registration (UIN number), third-party drone insurance policy, and complete a basic identity verification step. Once approved, you can toggle online status and accept local jobs.

The platform supports spraying of DGCA-approved agricultural nutrients, soluble bio-fertilizers, organic pest controllers, neem oil formulations, and standard crop protection chemicals. Farmers are advised to provide the chemicals as prescribed by agricultural officers, and our certified pilots will manage the water dilution and spray parameters.
